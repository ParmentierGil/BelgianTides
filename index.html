Belgian Tides

Check the sea levels

Belgian Tides gives you a visual representation of the sea levels on the Belgian Coast. Sea levels affect the amount of space available on the beach, which has shown to be important to ensure social distancing. Belgian Tides will be live in the next couple months and will be updated with all major Belgian coast cities.
